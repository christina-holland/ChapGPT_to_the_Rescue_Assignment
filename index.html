Understanding the Sequence

The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, starting from 0 and 1. It goes like this: 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, and so on.

Practical Applications

The Fibonacci sequence finds applications in various fields such as mathematics, computer science, biology, and finance. It's used in algorithms, modeling, and even in design and art.

Fibonacci in Nature

One of the most intriguing aspects of the Fibonacci sequence is its prevalence in nature. From the arrangement of petals in flowers to the spiral patterns of shells, Fibonacci numbers can be found everywhere in the natural world.
